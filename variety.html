Variety Notes

Variety P-102a represents the first issue of the 100 Baht Fourteenth Series note, released 18 November 2004. It bears the signatures of Governor Pridiyathorn Devakula and Finance Minister Somkid Jatusripitak.

Prefix ranges run from 0A to 3Z. The most common of the three P-102 varieties and regularly found in circulated condition.
