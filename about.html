About the Catalogue

A scholarly reference for Thai currency history

Purpose

The Thai Banknotes Catalogue is a comprehensive digital archive documenting the evolution of Thai currency from the First Series of 1880 to the present-day Seventeenth Series. This resource serves scholars, collectors, and enthusiasts interested in the monetary and cultural history of Thailand.

Scope

The catalogue encompasses all regular issue banknotes produced by the Bank of Thailand, including commemorative issues, signature varieties, and notable printing variations. Each entry provides detailed specifications, historical context, and authentication references.

Methodology

Information has been compiled from archival records, museum collections, and numismatic literature. All data undergoes peer review to ensure accuracy and scholarly integrity.
